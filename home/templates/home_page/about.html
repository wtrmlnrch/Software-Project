About Us

What is ThinkEat?

At ThinkEat, we empower individuals to transform their available ingredients into delicious, easy-to-follow recipes using OpenAI's ChatGPT models. Our mission is to help people make the most of the food they have, reducing waste and promoting sustainable consumption. Food waste in America is a significant issue, with approximately 38% of all food in America going unsold or uneaten each year, equating to 92 billion pounds of waste. Additionally, Americans spend an average of $166 per month per person on dining out. Costs like that add up very quickly. By providing personalized recipes based on the ingredients you already have, ThinkEat aims to reduce food waste and help you save money. Join us in creating a more sustainable and cost-effective approach to cooking and dining.
