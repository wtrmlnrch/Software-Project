About Us

What is ThinkEat?

At ThinkEat, we empower individuals to transform their available ingredients into delicious, easy-to-follow recipes using OpenAI's ChatGPT models. Our mission is to help people make the most of the food they have, reducing waste and promoting sustainable consumption.

Food waste in America is a significant issue, with approximately 38% of all food going unsold or uneaten each year, equating to 92 billion pounds of waste. By providing personalized recipes based on the ingredients you already have, ThinkEat aims to reduce food waste and help you save money. Join us in creating a more sustainable approach to cooking and dining.

Powered by OpenAI

ThinkEat is powered by OpenAI’s cutting-edge ChatGPT models, harnessing the latest advancements in AI to deliver a seamless and intelligent cooking experience. Our system takes your listed ingredients and dynamically searches for the most relevant and practical recipes, ensuring that every suggestion is both accessible and creative to our users. Unlike traditional recipe searches, ThinkEat doesn’t just pull static results—it intelligently understands context, flavor profiles, and cooking techniques, curating personalized recommendations tailored specifically to your available ingredients. By leveraging ChatGPT’s ability to aggregate knowledge from across the web, we provide access to an extensive and ever-growing repertoire of recipes—from Michelin-star chefs to hidden gems from niche culinary communities. This AI-driven approach allows ThinkEat to continuously refine its recommendations, offering a mix of classic comfort foods, quick weekday meals, and innovative dishes you may have never considered—all designed to minimize waste and maximize flavor. With ThinkEat, every meal is an opportunity to explore, create, and savor, using the power of AI to turn what’s in your kitchen into something extraordinary.

Meet the Team

Behind ThinkEat is a group of passionate individuals dedicated to making a difference. From developers to food enthusiasts, our team is committed to creating a platform that inspires sustainable cooking.
